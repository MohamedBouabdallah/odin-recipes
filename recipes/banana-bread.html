Banana Bread

Home

Description

A soft, moist banana bread that’s quick to make and perfect for breakfast or a snack. No mixer needed!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g sugar (white or brown)
  • 80 g melted butter (or 80 ml oil)
  • 200 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mash the bananas in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, sugar, and melted butter, then mix well.
  4. Stir in flour, baking powder, and salt until just combined.
  5. Add extras if desired (chocolate, nuts…).
  6. Pour into a greased loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 45–50 minutes (check with a knife).
  8. Let it cool before removing from the pan.
