Veggie Wrap

Home

Description

A fresh and healthy vegetarian wrap, perfect for a light lunch or dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • Lettuce
  • 2 tbsp hummus or cream cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Warm the tortillas slightly.
  2. Spread hummus or cream cheese.
  3. Slice the vegetables.
  4. Add everything to the wrap.
  5. Roll, cut in half, and serve.
